About Me


What's up! My name is Jarod Najera and I'm a 4th year Computer Science major at UC Riverside. Currently, I'm employed under UCR Residential Life as a member of Summer Team Norm for Highlander Orientation and will be an RA for Glen Mor during the upcoming school year.

When I first arrived at UCR, my heart was set on robotics. I fell in love with programming and building robots my senior year of high school when I joined my school's robotics team. There, I won an award for Rookie of the Year. My freshman year at UCR, I joined IEEE@UCR where I later became the org's Robotics Coordinator for the 2020-2021 academic year. As Robotics Coordinator, I led the org's MicroMouse division and taught workshops on various topics such as electronics basics, soldering, working with Arduinos, schematic design, etc.

Recently however, I've realized that my passion lies in web and app development, and I've been learning all that I can about it. This past year, I've worked on different projects to expand on my knowledge which you can check out here!

On my free time, I like to cook, code, make stuff, and play guitar. I currently play guitar for a band.
